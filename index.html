Serble

Hello and welcome everybody to Serble! a multi gamemode server fitted for every kind of player! Whether you like parkour, Bedwars, KitPVP, or even just a regular survival server! we have them and many more! We also have custom combat to make sure everything you find of this server is unique and awesome! Be sure to check up with both the Minecraft server And this discord server as updates will be coming left right and centre! Have fun and see you in the game.

We support 1.8 to 1.16.3 java/bedrock edition

Join Our Discord!

Gamemodes we offer:


Gen

Start by punching wood, goto a villager to get better tools until your a fully enchanted netherite.



Survival

Plain old anarchy survival. You are free to hack, grief, or even make a secret hidden base!



Bedwars

BedWars is a strategic PVP game with many styles that can lead you to victory or defeat! You must protect your bed, whilst trying to kill the enemies, without the bed, you cannot respawn!

Skywars/Hunger Games

Get the chests, then run and fight othes in an battle to survive! We put this under Skywars and Hunger Games because they are basically the same, its just skywars is in the air. In the server under skywars you will find maps that can be called both.        



KitPVP

Choose a kit and battle other, when you kill them you get healed and fed. How long can you survive  



Skyblock

Join the skyblock server for an epic skyblock experience, do /is to create your island and then build to your hearts conten, with custom cobble generaters and a shop with everything, you will never get bored
